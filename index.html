About SBBPSSSIC

Today, the role of a school is not only to pursue academic excellence, but also to inspire and empower its students to be lifelong learners, critical thinkers and productive members of an ever-changing global society.

Sardar Ballabhbhai Patel Memorial Educational Institute Rampur Ayodhya is located in one of the healthiest parts of UP. Hence an ideal institution for parents/guardians for whom the health of their children is a matter of prime importance. Its grand building is equipped with electric hall and learning environment, with its halls, well-stocked library, science laboratories, reading rooms, spacious rooms and facilities for football, hockey, cricket, basket ball, volleyball, badminton, kho-kho and There is a big playground for. The college can compare favorably with the best institute in UTTAR PRADESH.

The school has been continuously striving to provide quality education, inculcate discipline as well as opportunity for all-round personal development to its students. We are fortunate to have a dedicated, committed and capable faculty, supported by a motivated and cooperative non-teaching staff.
